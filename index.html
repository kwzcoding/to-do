How It Works

Responsive

To-Do is easy to use, navigate, and control. Just fill out a To-Do List of things you would like to do each day or week, and the times you want it done by. We will help give reminders and let you know if you are on track to finishing your goals for the day.

Flexible

To-Do will become a calender you cannot live without. Keep track of your progress. Add smart reminders so you never forget a thing.

Simple

Organize your tasks, lists and reminders in one easy to use app. To-Do syncs seamlessly across all your devices.

Support

Any time you need help with the website; whether you have a question about how it works, or just want to reach out, we have a support member waiting to help you.
